# Wenger Field Notes — Launch Guide (for the team)

A mobile-first app for logging California private-school sales visits.
Use it on your phone in the field.

## The app

**URL:** https://valkolimark-wenger-field-notes.vercel.app

Add it to your phone's home screen for quick access (Share → Add to
Home Screen on iPhone; ⋮ → Add to Home screen on Android).

## First login (everyone)

1. Open the URL. Enter your **work email** (e.g.
   `firstname.lastname@wengercorp.com`) and the temporary password:

   **`Wenger2026!`**

2. You'll be asked to **set your own password** (minimum 8
   characters). Choose something only you know.
3. You're in. Future logins use your email + your new password.

If your email isn't recognized, you're not on the roster yet — ask
Mark or Jackie to add you.

## The four screens

- **Map** — all 47 schools as pins. Search by name, filter by tier,
  tap a pin → school preview → **Start visit** to open the form.
- **Visit form** — fill in the priority block (Visit priority is the
  only required field), plus Contact / Purchasing / Decision-making /
  Marketing / Notes. Your progress auto-saves as a draft on that
  phone. Tap **Save submission** when done.
- **My Submissions** — every visit you've logged. Tap one to see the
  full read-only detail.
- **Account** — tap your name (top-right) → **Account** to change your
  name, email, or password. Email/password changes take effect at your
  next login.

Admins (Mark, Jackie) also see an **Admin dashboard** in that top-right
menu.

## Troubleshooting

- **Forgot your password?** Ask Mark or Jackie to reset it. You'll log
  in again with `Wenger2026!` and be asked to set a new one.
- **Changed your email?** Use the new email at your next login.
- **Something looks broken?** Refresh the page. If it persists, tell
  Mark.
- **Drafts** are saved on the specific phone/browser you used — they
  don't sync. Submitted visits *do* sync (they're saved to the
  database) and show up for admins.
